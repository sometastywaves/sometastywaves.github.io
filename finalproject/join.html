Membership Levels and Benefits

Non-Profit

Non-Profit organizations help out our community! Benefits include: Ad on local newspaper and full directory of local businesses and owner contact information.


Bronze

Benefits: Non-Profit benefits + monthly social media post, your company logo on select events sponsor images, and small ad on our website.


Silver

Benefits: Bronze + medium ad on our site, company logo on all event sponsor images, and (1 week) annual feature of your company on our homepage.


Gold

Benefits: Silver + professional photoshoot of your business, (4 different weeks of the year) special feature on our homepage, large logo on all event sponsor images, and access to event and marketing meetings at our location.
