Design Principles

Repetition

Mollusk Surf Shop

Upon looking at their mobile, categories are convienently organized with the same size, font, and contrast. The background images also follows the rule of thirds. This it a good example of using repetition to both order categories and have visual attention of them.

White Space

Craigslist

This site uses white space to highlight the options that the user will be most likely be using on the site. The white space allows simplicity and helps to focus particular and important aspects of the website.

Contrast

Field Notes

This site uses contrast on their 2 front images on the home page. Almost like a ying-yang symbol, the 2 images feature lighter and darker colors, and contrasting font colors. They balance well, bring attention to what products these images which to show.
