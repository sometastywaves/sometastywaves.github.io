Target Audience

Our audience will be the general public, from ages 8+ to 65. We will have our site be as user friendly as possible, so that it will be easily read by youth and adults, yet include more specific and techinal weather details for those who might need it for more professional reasons.

Persona #1

Working Adults

One of the primary users of the site will be working adults(male and female), specifically from the age group of 25-45. They will be using this site as either a weekly or daily source for weather that is understandable and clear to read.Their goal in using this site will be to help prepare and gear for their commute in the following days.

Persona #2

Middle and High Schoolers

Another large group who will use this site, will be school aged youth (12-18).They will most likely be this site daily for its easy to read layout. Their goal is to use this site to plan their outfit for the day, since youth often take more consideration choosing what to wear.

Scenario #1

A delivery driver sees dark clouds and wants to know what to wear during his deliveries.

Scenario #2

A local farmer wants to know how the weather will be for the next 2 weeks so he can plan the irrigation accordingly.

Scenario #3

A high schooler got a new winter outfit and wants to wear it the first snow of the season, so he need to know what day it will be.

Scenario #4

A group of students built a solar powered oven for their science class and need to find a sunny day during the week to test out their project.

Scenario #5

It's summer and the local park ranger would like to know the temperature and wind of the day to determine a fire risk.
