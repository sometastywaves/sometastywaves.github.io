Color Scheme for Sweater Weather Report

Overall Color Choice

Blue and Orange are often used in meteorology, with blue referring to the sky and orange to signal inclement weather.

Mustard and Orange

Mustard will be used for the background color outside of the main content. It will help and complement the blue colors that will be used for content boxes. The orange will be used to highlight the nav list that will be useful to the user.

Indigo Dye and Queen Blue

Indigo Dye will be the main color for the main content. Because of its association with weather (blue skies), it give character to the pages.The Queen Blue will help complement the indigo and orange/yellow colors for the box around some of the titles.
